Dragon Care Guide

Basic Dragon Care Requirements

All dragons require daily feeding, exercise, and social interaction. Fire-breathing dragons need fireproof housing and proper ventilation. Budget at least $1,000 monthly for food and care supplies.

Feeding Guidelines

Dragons are carnivores and require fresh meat daily. Small dragons eat 20-30 lbs, medium dragons 40-60 lbs, and large dragons 80-100 lbs of meat per day. Always provide fresh water.

Training and Socialization

Begin training early with positive reinforcement. Dragons bond strongly with their handlers. Regular flight time is essential for physical and mental health. Consider professional training classes for beginners.

Legal Requirements

Dragon ownership requires a special exotic pet license in most regions. Fire-breathing dragons require additional permits. Check your local regulations before adopting.

Health and Veterinary Care

Annual check-ups with a dragon veterinarian are required. Common health issues include scale rot, wing injuries, and digestive problems. Emergency vet services should be available 24/7.
